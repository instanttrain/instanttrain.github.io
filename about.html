Welcome to the Portfolio of Taylor Eruysal

I’m Taylor Eruysal, a game designer and artist. I'm currently attending Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, with an Electronic Arts concentration in the Games and Simulation Arts and Sciences program. My specialties are 3D art and game design, although I also have experience in rigging, animation, pixel art, music composition, and level design as well.

My work at RPI has included many team projects with specific requirements and deadlines. As a result, I’m flexible with artistic mediums, and I focus on the fundamentals of a product first and foremost.

I believe every team needs members that can act as a mediator within an artistic pipeline, and create products from a broad design viewpoint. My interests are always growing (I’m currently really interested in studying lighting and scene composition, for example), and I want to help create quality products that look and feel like no one else’s.

This website contains the very best of my work. I hope you enjoy it.
