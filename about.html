A little about me...

Taylor Eruysal is a American video game designer, artist, and animator. Taylor is currently attending Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute for a Bachelor of Science degree in the Games and Simulation Arts and Sciences program, with a concentration in Electronic Arts. He primarily uses Photoshop, Maya, and Unity to make his games, but has worked with a variety of other programs such as GameMaker and FireAlpaca.

Taylor has participated in a variety of groups, from school-funded research teams to small labours-of-love. His work has been featured with the RPI Summer Undergraduate Research Program, and RPI Gamefest. His weaknesses include French lop rabbits, pepperoni pizza, and muted pastels.

Taylor lives in Latham, NY. He can be contacted at tayloreruysal@gmail.com.
