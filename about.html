About

Hello, I'm Taylor Eruysal.

I'm a game designer, artist, and content creator.

My design philosophy

Gameplay is story, story is gameplay - the experience is the narrative

The act of playing a game is storytelling, and a game should give the player ways to make that story their own - choices, surprises, and generosity. Appeal to the heart!

Central mechanics need thoughtful support - reinforce them

Encourage the player to use central game mechanics with interactivity, reward, and granularity in the way they traverse the ingame space. Constantly remind yourself what the game is really about, and what makes it unique.

Scope responsibly, but dream with ambition

As a solo developer, understanding the reality of time and productivity requirements a project needs is key to being prolific - and a great way to think about designing to your strengths. That being said, making games is fun, and trying to push the envelope helps move the industry forward, too.
