Welcome to the Portfolio of Taylor Eruysal

Taylor Eruysal is an American video game designer, artist, animator, and musician. He is currently attending Rensselaer Polytecnic Institute and is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Games and Simulation Arts and Sciences with a concentration in Electronic Arts. Taylor mostly works with Maya, Photoshop, and Unity, but also has worked with other programs including GameMaker and FireAlpaca.

In his free time, Taylor has taught guest lectures at his university, written music, and participated in a variety of groups, from school-funded research teams to small labours-of-love. His work has been featured with the RPI Summer Undergraduate Research Program and RPI Gamefest.
