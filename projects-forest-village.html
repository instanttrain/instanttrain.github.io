Forest Village (Unreal Engine)

This was a showcase piece designed to create a stylized environment in Unreal Engine 4.

Rustic environments are always such a pleasure to work in - it always feels like sculpting together a tiny diorama with all the natural touches like blades of grass. I enjoyed scripting the camera's movement through the scene; it was reminiscent of coding a rail-shooter environment.
