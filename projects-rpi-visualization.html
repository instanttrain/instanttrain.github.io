RPI Visualizations

In 2019, I was hired by Dr. Runye Zha to create illustrations to be used in presentations and business proposals involving the studies of the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering.

The work allowed me to think about scale and abstraction in a unique manner. It was a bit of a balancing act to choose colors for elements like proteins with an artistic flair, while still maintaining an informative feel that contextualized the higher-level concepts.
