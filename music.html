Music


When I'm not making games or creating visual art, I also make chiptune music. Generally I specialize in video game covers with Famitracker, although I occassionally make something different every now and then. I've also taught several guest lectures at my university about game music and NES sound functionality. Here's a link to a 2016 workshop I taught. All my source files are always available free of charge.
