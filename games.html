Post-School Projects (2017 - )

Forest Village - 2018

This was a showcase created specifically to demonstrate my environment/asset art running inside a game engine - specifically Unreal Engine 4. It features texture painting, 60 FPS framerate, and a full asset breakdown.

Role: Designer, Artist, Composer, Programmer/Scripter

Quill (Name Pending) - 2018

I contributed item and background art for the Quill visual novel engine. I used compositing techniques, outline rendering, and paintovers to create the necessary atmosphere.

School Projects (2013 - 2017)

AllCorp and You - 2016

A visual novel exploring the role of technology in one's life. Use your smartphone's health and lifestyle app to help you meet new people, and navigate social currents.

Space Duck: A Game About a Duck in Space - 2016

A 3D puzzle-adventure designed for families.

Control Space Duck as he explores the planet his spaceship crashed on, avoid robots, and interact with switches and bridges of light.

Features illustrated cutscenes, multiple puzzle mechanics, and custom shaders.

Role: Game Designer, 3D Artist, Character Designer, Level Designer

Download Link

Polterheist - 2016

A heist-puzzle game about a supernatural thief performing a heist inside a ghostly robotic tomb.

The protagonist, Vilette, can interact with the spirits of the dead, granting her new abilities like invisibility and teleportation.

The player must sneak around the mausoleum, evade guards, and use their abilities to navigate the mazelike hallways.

Role: Gameplay Designer, 3D Artist/Animator, Music Composer.

Knowledge Lab - 2015

Made for the RPI Summer Undergraduate Research Project of 2015 under the supervision of Dr. Marc Destefano.

An educational game for fourth graders, created with the NYS Common Core curriculum in mind.

Knowledge Lab has the player exploring a vast land of robots to recreate hard drive data stolen by a rogue robot.

Features over 8 educational minigames, a number of colorful areas and characters.

Role: Creative Director, Writer, Gameplay Designer, Artist.
